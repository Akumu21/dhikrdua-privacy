Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Dhikr & Dua ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app may request access to your device's location solely to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. This location data is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Location Permission

Location access is used only to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your area using the Aladhan API
  • Determine the Qibla direction from your current position

Your location is sent to the Aladhan API (aladhan.com) to retrieve prayer times. Please refer to their privacy policy at aladhan.com.

Data Storage

All app data (settings, preferences, dhikr counts) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — for prayer time calculations

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
